WHO I AM

Oakland born and raised, I’ve kept music and artists at the center of my life since I was a kid. My mission is simple: replenish artists and music organizations with new possibilities in the studio and beyond. I geek out on helping creators turn raw creativity into real audience connection. Long term, I’m committed to helping transform the music industry into something more compassionate, equitable, and sustainable.

I’ve been fortunate to create alongside some of the best, including Grateful Dead, Bob Weir, G-Eazy, Lukas Nelson, Alphabet Rockers, Slightly Stoopid, and The National, among many others. I’ve also connected major platforms such as Amazon, MTV, Airbnb, HBO, and Jack Daniels to original music and emerging voices.

I’m an active coach to creators worldwide and co-developed the nonprofit accelerator ZOOLABS, supporting hundreds of artists and startups as they align their art, business, and purpose. In 2021, I began building the next generation song collaboration platform for artists and fans, Songfinch.

Today, I live in Nashville, working with artists, growing a YouTube channel, and continuing to open new faucets of possibility for music creators and facilitators everywhere.
