BRAD DOLLAR

Music producer. Guitarist. Creative operator.

As an Oakland Bay Area native, I've been cultivating sound and building connections through music since my youth. My core mission is to replenish artists with new possibilities in the studio and equip them with everything needed to translate creativity into connection with real audiences. Long-term, I strive to help the music industry transcend into a more compassionate, equitable, and sustainable existence.

With a passion for creating the conditions that allow music and people to grow, I've produced and engineered tracks with Bob Weir (Grateful Dead), Malik Yusef (Beyoncé), G-Eazy, Alphabet Rockers (GRAMMY winning), Slightly Stoopid, and The National, among many others, and connected platforms like Amazon, MTV, Airbnb, HBO, and Jack Daniels to fresh content.

I'm also an active coach to music creators worldwide, including co-developing and growing the non-profit music accelerator ZOOLABS, helping hundreds of musicians and music startups align art, business, and purpose. In 2021, I began building the artist services and development branch of Songfinch. Today, I live in Nashville and continue catalyzing artists in creative spaces around the planet.

What I Do

With the direct support of over 1000 released titles and hundreds of artists and startups, I'm a lifetime music builder dedicated to creating meaningful and lasting content paired with revolutionary strategies and campaigns.

My strongest ability is creating a positive, productive, and powerful environment that translates through speakers to those you're trying to reach. I activate creators to craft in a way that inspires people and content to be their best while leaving a deep and lasting impression on audiences across time.
