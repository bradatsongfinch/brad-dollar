Who I Am

Oakland Bay Area born and raised, I've kept music and artists at the center of my life since I was a kid. My mission is simple: replenish artists and music organizations with new possibilities in the studio and beyond. I geek out on equipping others to turn creativity into real audience connection.

From playing guitar in ska-punk bands to working in the biggest recording studios in the world — I've been lucky to make music with the best. Grateful Dead, Bob Weir, G-Eazy, Lukas Nelson, Alphabet Rockers (GRAMMY winners), Slightly Stoopid, The National, and many others.

I'm an active coach to creators worldwide and co-developed the nonprofit accelerator ZOOLABS, helping hundreds of artists and startups align their art, business, and purpose. In 2021, began building Songfinch — the next gen song collaboration platform for artists and fans.

Long term, I'm going to do everything I can to help transform the music industry into something more compassionate, equitable, and sustainable. Reach out anytime — I'd love to help you get where you want to go with your music.
